2. Executive Summary
VoltAdvance is a niche, utility-grade credit clearing network that decouples a borrower's mobile identity (MSISDN) from the physical infrastructure asset (the prepaid electricity meter).
The platform sits directly inside the transaction stream of private sub-metering providers and municipal integrators via real-time webhooks. This position allows it to enforce automated debt recovery from future electricity purchases, regardless of the channel (banking apps, retail tills, or informal spaza shops) used by the tenant.
