1. Executive Summary
VoltAdvance enables prepaid electricity customers to receive small emergency electricity advances before payday.
Unlike traditional lending solutions, advances are attached to the electricity meter rather than the individual customer.
Repayment occurs automatically from the next electricity purchase regardless of where that purchase is made, provided the purchase flows through participating vending networks.
